The Solution

Monthly, invite-only event in SF. ~50 people, ~3 hours. Ten structured 15-minute 1-on-1s per person, with pre-matching based on profiles.

Institutional Leaders (10-15)

Funders, hiring managers, program directors. People who can hire, fund, or place talent.

Top Talent (30-35)

BlueDot's highest-rated alumni. Invited based on course performance and facilitator recommendations.

The Specific Outcome

Every attendee leaves with at least one concrete opportunity they wouldn't have found otherwise.

  • Job interviews, offers, and hires traced to event connections
  • Funding applications submitted
  • Collaborations and projects started

Tracked through facilitator follow-up calls, not just surveys. Evidence from EAGx Australia shows surveys alone don't capture real impact at 6 months.
